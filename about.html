About the Artists

Trent Ducombs is an independent producer and multi-instrumentalist from New Orleans Louisiana. He has played in several brass and metal bands and began his career playing saxophone in high school and marching in 2nd lines around the greater NOLA area. He has covered many genres from the blues to doom metal and electronic music and has released 2 studio projects so far, being the Snowfall EP from his Retrograde duo and the band Boisvert's self titled album. Retrograde is a Metal/ R&B duo formed by founding members Trent Ducombs and Max Anderson. Their first EP Snowfall combines classic doom and drone metal sounds like Electric Wizard Devil's Witches and inspired by jazz and hip hop grooves from artists like JDilla and Massive Attack.
